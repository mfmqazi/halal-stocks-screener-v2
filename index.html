Screening Criteria

Our comprehensive approach to Shariah-compliant and ethical investing

Shariah Compliance

  • Interest-based income < 5%
  • Total debt / market cap < 33%
  • Liquid assets / market cap < 33%
  • Accounts receivable / market cap < 49%
  • No alcohol, gambling, pork, or tobacco
  • No conventional banking or insurance

Ethical Screening

  • No support for Israeli occupation
  • No weapons manufacturing for oppression
  • No companies on BDS list
  • Fair labor practices required
  • Environmental responsibility
  • Human rights compliance

Financial Health

  • Positive earnings growth
  • Strong balance sheet
  • Consistent revenue streams
  • Reasonable P/E ratios
  • Market capitalization > $500M
  • Average daily volume > 100K

About Halal Stocks

Halal Stocks is a comprehensive platform dedicated to helping Muslim investors make informed, Shariah-compliant investment decisions while maintaining strong ethical standards.

We go beyond traditional Shariah screening by also filtering out companies that support oppression, particularly those complicit in the genocide in Palestine. Our ethical framework ensures your investments align with Islamic values and human rights principles.

Our screening methodology is based on AAOIFI standards and incorporates additional ethical criteria developed in consultation with Islamic scholars and human rights organizations.
